Jan. 14th: A How-To Blog

How not to get captured as a PR rep

So far my methods of avoiding capture have been very successful. I have decided that I will share some of these methods with my blog readers so that they too can be as successful. The main weapon that we have developed for the Computer Engineering Arsenal that I would like to share is called "The Leash". The leash works by limiting one thing: carelessness. For anyone who does not know, the PR reps for each team have to stay within arm's reach of a team-mate or they can be captured by another team. It is easy to stay within arms reach of a team-mate if you are paying attention, but as soon as you get careless and wander away, an opponent can strike. This can be a pretty big issue if you are drunk. The leash is a simple tether that can keep this from happening.

Steps to success


Step 1:

Attach two Lanyards Together


Step 2:

Attach the lanyards to yourself


Step 3:

Add carabiner to the end of the lanyards


Step 4:

Find a friendly team-mate to help you out


Step 5:

Attach the carabiner to that team-mate




Congratulations!!! You will now be safe no matter how drunk you get.

Warning: Friendships may be destroyed with the use of this product.
