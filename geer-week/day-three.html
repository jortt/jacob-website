Day Three

Jan. 15th: Half-Way There

Visitors in the night

Last night while doing some late night blogging, there was a knock on the door. I went to answer it and I saw no body. This was very odd because you would think that there must be a person at the door in order for someone to knock. I shut the door, thinking that I was imagining things. Then there was another knock on the door but still I could not see a person. Using these two observations I came to a conclusion:

(I could not see a person) ^ (a person must have been at the door) ==> the person must be invisible.

After realizing this I let the invisible man into the room. We had a very nice conversation about the previous days activities. He never said anything in this conversation, but I can only assume that he was listening because he texted me a picture (as seen on the right) part way through the conversation. It was sent with the message "we see you". I can now finally sleep relaxed knowing that there is a group of invisible men looking over me in the club room hallway. I have faith that they will keep me safe from all harm. I am also hoping that I can get some information regarding the penguins from these men. They may be the answer to all of the riddles that ETLC has been throwing at me ever since I moved in.

On the topic of bathrooms and showers

My shower schedule has been compromised. This morning I got out of the club room to have a shower and found someone hiding and waiting for me. Luckily I guessed that it would occur and arranged an escort in advance. It was about time that someone caught on. I will either have to completely rethink my shower schedule for the rest of the week or have an escort every time from now on. I still have not been captured but I am starting to think that my luck is starting to run out. We will see what the next two days have in store.
